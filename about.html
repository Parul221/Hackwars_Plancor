About us

Our Mission

The innovation involved in transforming waste materials into new products can spur the growth of new industries and business models, leading to job creation.

Promoting and adopting products made from reused materials fosters a culture of sustainability and social responsibility.

Our Vision

Recycling and making sustainable products is an existing market but all the products made revolves around some specific things like plates, cups, packing boxes.

There is need of product innovation in this field. We will be making different decorative and gifting products using materials like bagasse, rice husk banana stem, hemp, dry flowers and more.
